#### 84th EAGE Annual Conference & Exhibition, Vienna, Austria (2023)

Presented two PC-based oral presentations at the 84th EAGE Annual Conference & Exhibition in Vienna, Austria:

- *Unsupervised Learning Framework for Denoising Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) Data*
- *Unsupervised Learning with a Soft Attention Mechanism for 3-D Erratic and Random Noise Attenuation*

These presentations focused on unsupervised deep learning approaches for DAS data denoising and 3-D seismic noise attenuation.
