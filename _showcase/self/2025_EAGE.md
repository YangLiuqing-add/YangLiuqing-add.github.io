#### 🇫🇷 86th EAGE Annual Conference & Exhibition, Toulouse, France (2025)

Presented the conference paper **“Unsupervised Learning with Frequency-Guided Constraints for Improved Hardrock Seismic Imaging”** at the 86th EAGE Annual Conference & Exhibition held in Toulouse, France.

The work introduced an unsupervised learning framework incorporating frequency-guided constraints to enhance seismic imaging quality in hardrock environments, with a focus on preserving geological structures while suppressing acquisition-related noises.
