#### 🇬🇧 87th EAGE Annual Conference & Exhibition, Aberdeen, United Kingdom (2026)

Presented **“Physics-Informed Unsupervised Deconvolution for High-Fidelity Hardrock Seismic Imaging”** at the 87th EAGE Annual Conference & Exhibition in Aberdeen, United Kingdom.

The study introduced a physics-informed unsupervised framework for seismic deconvolution, integrating geological and wave-propagation constraints to improve resolution and fidelity in hardrock seismic imaging. The work aimed to enhance reflector continuity and structural interpretability while preserving physically meaningful subsurface information.
